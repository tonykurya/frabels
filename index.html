Who We Are

Frabels Garment House is a leading Textile and Mass production Apparel Manufacturer. Locally owned, we pride ourselves on running a world class apparel sourcing, design and manufacturing company. We pride ourselves on quality, sustainability, ethical business and fair labour practices.

Our Philosophy

We believe that garments should be inspiring pieces that influence the wearers and their daily life. We care about improving the wearing experience of our consumers and providing nicely done durable and functional products.

Why Choose Us

By being able to produce 450,000 units of apparels per month with the latest technology on critical operations, we are proud that the output adheres to all the standards set by our valued clientelle.

We are committed to excellence and our philosophy is governed by highly developed attention to detail in every stage of our process.
